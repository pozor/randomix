## ğŸš€ Tips & Tricks

### Number Generator
- **Tip:** Turn off "Allow repetitions" to exhaust all numbers in a range
- **Tip:** Use History sorting to find min/max generated values
- **Tip:** Previous number display helps avoid accidental re-rolls

### Phrase Generator
- **Tip:** Assign weight=10 to frequently wanted items, weight=1 to rare items
- **Tip:** Probability = weight / total_weights (e.g., 5/(5+2+3) = 50%)
- **Tip:** Swipe to delete phrases quickly
