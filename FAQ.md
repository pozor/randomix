# FAQ

## General

**Is Pocket Random free?**
Yes, completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.

**Does it work offline?**
Yes, Pocket Random works 100% offline.

**What devices are supported?**
iPhone/iPad with iOS 13.4 or later.

---

## Privacy

**Is my data private?**
Yes. All data stays on your device. Nothing is collected or sent anywhere.

**What happens if I uninstall?**
All data is permanently deleted.

---

## Number Generator

**What does "Allow repetitions" do?**
- ON: Same number can appear multiple times
- OFF: Each number appears only once until all are used

**How do I clear history?**
Tap the history icon (top left) → "Clear History"

---

## Phrase Generator

**How do weights work?**
Higher weight = more likely to be selected.

Example: Phrase A (weight 5) vs Phrase B (weight 5) = 50% each

**How do I delete a phrase?**
Swipe left on the phrase → Delete

---

## Distributions

**What distributions are available?**
Uniform, Normal, Exponential, Poisson, Binomial, Weibull, Triangular

---

## Settings

**How do I change theme/language?**
Go to Settings tab and select your preference.

**Do settings persist?**
Yes, all settings are saved automatically.

---

## Shake-to-Generate

**How do I enable it?**
Settings tab → "Shake to Generate" → ON

**Why doesn't it trigger immediately after shaking?**
There's a 1-second cooldown to prevent multiple triggers.

---

## Troubleshooting

**App crashes on launch**
1. Force quit and reopen
2. Restart device
3. Reinstall app (deletes data)

**VoiceOver not working correctly**
[Report an accessibility issue](https://github.com/pozor/pocket-random/issues/new?template=bug_report.md)
